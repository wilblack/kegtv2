Keg T.V. is a billboard application for breweries and beer halls. Billboards are designed to run on flat screen T.V.'s and display a list of beers the bar serves. The bar can choose what beers are show on a billboard and change them as needed. Each billboard contains a Menu and Menus have set of Menu Items. Menu Items are basically a beer and infomation about the beer such as beer type (IPA, Amber Ale, Stout, etc...), who brewed it, and where.
